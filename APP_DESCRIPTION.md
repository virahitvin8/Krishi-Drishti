# Krishi Drishti - App Description for Google Play Store

## Short Description (80 characters max)
See your farm health from space. Free satellite-based crop monitoring for Indian farmers.

## Full Description

**Krishi Drishti** (कृषि दृष्टि) brings the power of satellites directly to Indian farmers.

Using free data from Sentinel-2 and other satellites, our app analyses your farm and gives you clear, simple insights about your crop health — without any technical knowledge required.

### Key Features:

✅ **Individual Analysis**  
Get clear percentages for NDVI, EVI, NDWI, REIP and more for your selected land.

✅ **Overall Health Score**  
See your farm’s health on a simple 0-100 scale with color-coded status.

✅ **Hotspot Map**  
View a transparent grid on your field showing exactly which areas are healthy (blue) or stressed (red).

✅ **Weather + Irrigation Advice**  
Get combined recommendations using satellite data + live weather.

✅ **Multi-Satellite Insights**  
Access data from Sentinel-2, Sentinel-1 (SAR), Landsat and more in the Advanced section.

✅ **Installable as App**  
Works as a Progressive Web App — install it directly on your phone.

### How it Works:
1. Select your land (draw on map, enter coordinates, or upload CSV)
2. Click "Analyze"
3. Get instant results with clear recommendations

### Perfect For:
- Small and marginal farmers
- Progressive farmers using precision agriculture
- FPOs and farmer groups
- Agricultural students and researchers
