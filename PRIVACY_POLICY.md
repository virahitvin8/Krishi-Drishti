# Privacy Policy for Krishi Drishti

**Effective Date:** June 02, 2026

**Krishi Drishti** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

### a. Personal Information
- Name, phone number, or email (if you choose to create an account)
- Farm location data (latitude, longitude, and polygon boundaries you provide)

### b. Usage Data
- Satellite analysis results for your selected land
- App usage patterns and preferences

### c. Device Information
- Device type, operating system, and browser information (for PWA functionality)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide crop health analysis based on satellite data for your selected land
- Generate personalized recommendations for irrigation, pest risk, etc.
- Improve the app experience and features
- Send important updates or alerts (only if you opt-in)

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We **do not sell** your personal data.

We may share data with trusted third-party services only when necessary:

- **Copernicus Data Space Ecosystem** (ESA) – for fetching satellite imagery
- **Supabase** – for secure data storage and authentication
- **Open-Meteo** – for weather data

These services have their own privacy policies.

## 4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
